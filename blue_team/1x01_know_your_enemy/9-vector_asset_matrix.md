## 2. High-Priority Intersections

### The 3 Most Connected Assets (Highest Exposure)
1. **Active Directory Domain Controllers:** Reachable by 7 out of 8 threat vectors, representing our most dangerous point of failure because domain takeover instantly grants total control over all integrated systems.
2. **EHR Database (`ehr-db-01`):** Reachable by 7 out of 8 threat vectors because our flat internal network allows any minor endpoint compromise to easily route directly to the core database on port 5432.
3. **Backup NAS (`nas-srv-01`):** Reachable by 7 out of 8 threat vectors, meaning attackers can easily target and destroy our primary recovery safety net as their first step during an attack.

### The 3 Most Versatile Vectors (Highest Utility)
1. **Insider (Negligent):** Able to reach all 7 critical assets because human errors, credential sharing, and shadow IT bypass all physical and technical perimeter firewalls daily.
2. **Insider (Malicious):** Able to reach all 7 critical assets due to holding trusted, authenticated access inside the corporate network, allowing them to bypass external security defenses.
3. **Phishing / Spear Phishing:** Able to reach 6 out of 7 critical assets because it acts as the universal catalyst that harvests administrative credentials, which can then be used to navigate our flat internal network topology.
